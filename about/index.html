About

Kyle is a Statistician + Data Scientist + Software Engineer on the Research Team at CDK Global in Seattle. He is often seen reading or muttering to himself in front of a whiteboard, but occasionally he builds something useful.

He graduated in 2015 with an MS in Statistics from the University of Washington. Before that, he spent some time as a Research Assistant at the Institute of Mathematics - Academia Sinica in Taipei.

Some of his less secretive work can be found on Github.

He also jointly runs a working group with some UW Statistics students.

Contact him at kyleclo-at-uw-dot-edu or on LinkedIn.
