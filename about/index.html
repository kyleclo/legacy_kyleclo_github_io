About

Welcome to my site!

I’m an Applied Research Scientist at the Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence (AI2) in Seattle. I just started so nothing to show yet!

I graduated in 2015 with an MS in Statistics from the University of Washington. Before that, I spent some time as a research assistant at the Institute of Mathematics - Academia Sinica in Taipei and a couple years as a statistician at CDK Global.

You can find examples of some of my work on Github.

I also jointly run a working group with some of my friends from the UW Statistics department. We’re currently interested in reinforcement learning and bandit problems, and you can find some of our code on Github.

Outside of work, I like playing tennis, watching movies, and playing video/tabletop games.

You can contact me at kyleclo-at-uw-dot-edu or on LinkedIn.
